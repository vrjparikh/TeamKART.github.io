Vacancies are available in the following subsystems:

Suspension
Chassis and Aerodynamics
Transmission

Corporate and Public Relations

Work description:

All: Need to work efficiently in a team to finish the tasks by the set deadline. Time Management.

Suspension:
Vehicle dynamics, Suspension geometry, Wheel Assembly (upright & hub) design & anti-roll bar design and manufacturing of said components.

Chassis and Aerodynamics:
Vehicle dynamics, Design, Simulation and Manufacture of Chassis and Body panels.

Transmission: Designing of differential assembly, sprocket, gearbox and pneumatic shifting mechanism.

Electronics: ECU programming, Design and fabrication of Power Distribution Module, Data Acquisition System, Driver Information Panel, Battery Management System.

CPR: Handling finances, Maintaining good relations with administration, corporate partners and the media, Handling Social media and Web presence
